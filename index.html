About the Game

Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock is a game that builds on traditional Rock, Paper, Scissors by adding two more choices for players. It was first seen on the TV show 'The Big Bang Theory', where two characters used the game to decide what to watch on TV.

How To Play

  1. Choose your weapon
    • Rock beats scissors and lizard
    • Paper beats rock and spock
    • Scissors beats paper and lizard
    • Lizard beats paper and spock
    • Spock beats rock and scissors
  2. Press Fight
  3. See the overall winner after 5 games
